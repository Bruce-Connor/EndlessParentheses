Endless Parentheses is a blog about Emacs. It features concise posts on improving your productivity and making Emacs life easier in general. Code included will be predominantly emacs-lisp, lying anywhere in the complexity spectrum with a blatant disregard for explanations or tutorials.

The outcome is that the posts read quickly and pleasantly for experienced Emacsers, while new enthusiasts are invited to digest the code and ask questions in the comments.
