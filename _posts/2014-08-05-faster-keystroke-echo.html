--- title: "Faster Keystroke Echo" date: 2014-08-05 layout: post tags: tip init.el published: false ---

My laptop is old it is almost getting out-performed by my smartphone. The principal consequence being that the keyboard has started to give up on life. Occasionally, a key won't register when I hit it, and occasionally I'll get paranoid and hit a key twice without knowing it actually had registered the first time.

Emacs' keystroke echoing is a fantastic aid in this regard, informing me whether the key worked or not. I only need to make it faster so that I don't have to wait for it.

{% highlight cl %} (setq echo-keystrokes 0.1) {% endhighlight %}
